    An Android client for Pump.io

    Project maintained by oshepherd Hosted on GitHub Pages — Theme by mattgraham

    What is it

    An early, quite incomplete client for Pump.io which runs on Android Ice Cream Sandwich and later.

    At present, it solves only ~10% of the problem: getting your feed and notifying you of updates. Fortunately, that happens to be the most important 10%.

    For my future directions, see this post on my feed

    To try it out, either check out and build the source or download a precompiled version
